Cleveland

Hi! Welcome to my page on Cleveland, my hometown. I am originally from a suburb of Cleveland called Pepper Pike, but I have worked in Downtown Cleveland, and done a lot of exploring. Through this page, you can see all of my adventures in downtown Cleveland, Chagrin Falls, and everywhere in between.

Cleveland has a lot of great attractions and sports teams. Cleveland is home to the Cleveland Clinic, a world-renowned hospital system. Cleveland is also home to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and the recently-renovated Flats District. Cleveland also boasts some incredible sports teams: the Cavaliers basketball team, the Browns football team, and the Indians baseball team. All of these great Cleveland staples are pictured below!
